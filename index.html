New
⬇

Built-in Video Downloader

Download videos from YouTube, TikTok, X, Vimeo, Instagram and 1000+ other sites directly inside Nova. Choose quality (4K, 1080p, 720p, audio-only) and pick your save folder.

New
🔄

Auto-Convert Any Format

Unsupported or incompatible video? Nova silently converts it with the bundled ffmpeg — no installs, no popups. A live progress bar keeps you informed while it works.

New
↩

Resume Interrupted Downloads

Lost your internet or had a power cut mid-download? Nova saves your queue to disk and lets you resume right where it left off — individually or all at once.

Fixed
🖱

"Open with" & Default Player

Nova now appears in Windows' "Open with" menu and can be set as your default video player system-wide, so double-clicking any video file opens it instantly.

🎬

All Major Formats

MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, WebM and more — plays them all without extra codecs.

📋

Playlist Management

Drag and drop multiple files, skip between videos, and auto-advance when one ends.

🖼

Picture in Picture

Keep watching while you work. Pop the video into a floating window with one click.

💾

Remembers Everything

Window size, position, and volume are saved automatically between sessions.

⚡

Fast & Lightweight

Launches instantly. No bloat, no splash screens, no unnecessary background processes.

🌙

Beautiful Dark UI

Sleek dark interface with smooth animations and controls that fade when not needed.
